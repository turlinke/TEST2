Our Story

GreenCut Lawn Care was born right here in Central Texas. What started as a teenager with a push mower and a handful of neighbors has grown into a trusted local lawn care service that families and businesses across the region count on week after week.

We've been mowing, trimming, and caring for Central Texas lawns for years — through the scorching summers, the unpredictable storms, and everything in between. We know this land, we know this climate, and we know what it takes to keep a yard looking great out here.
